Class: Bullet

Bullet(scene, speed-, parent, shootRange)

The Bullet class. The class where the properties of the bullets are generated. Including player shooting, enemy and tower shooting.

Constructor

new Bullet(scene, speed-, parent, shootRange)

sets up the bullets object. In the case of our project, the bullets that players, enemies and towers shoot out. Prepare the behavior and properties of bullets that are used.
